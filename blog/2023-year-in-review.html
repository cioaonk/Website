2023- A Year in Review

Last Updated: 1/15/2024 Twitter Back to my Main page


2023- What a year. As I look back at all this last year contained, one persistent theme comes to mind. 2023 was a year of incredible grouwth and progress for me in a number of ways. Graduating college, moving across the country and starting full time in a new job will also do that.


Here are the biggest highlights for me personally, in no order:

  • Earned CYSA+ by Comptia
  • Presented at and organized the 3rd BYU Cybersecurity Student Symposium
  • Completed my term as BYU Cybersecurity Student Association President
  • Moved out to Washington DC
  • Volunteered with CAMLIS
  • Attended SAINTCON
  • Volunteered with CPTC and supported the New England Regional
  • Started working full time with Peraton Labs

Other things I'm really excited about

  1. Made a bunch of new friends in DC
  2. Spent lots of time with family and friends
  3. Read more!

Lessons Learned:

  • Coding is tough but starting 5 minutes at a time is a great way to tackle any challenge.
  • Showing up is such an important skill.
  • You can both hit the ground running, and get your feet set under you after making a major change
  • Reflection and Orientation are the firsts steps towards actual real progress.

Overall I'm just grateful for how smooth, such a big transition this year has been. The number of unexpected opportunities that have come my way and led me to even greater experiences than I could have expected. I can truly say this year was one of the most I've grown, learned, and enjoyed. If you've been a part of this, thank you- from the bottom of my heart.
